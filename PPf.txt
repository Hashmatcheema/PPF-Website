Software Requirements Specification For
Pak-Palestine Forum (PPF) Website (Phase-1)
Version 1.0
Prepared by: Dev Team
Date: 08/02/26
1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose
This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) document defines the functional and non-functional requirements for the Pak-Palestine Forum (PPF) public website. The purpose of this document is to clearly describe the scope, features, and constraints of Phase-1 of the website to guide designers, developers, testers, and stakeholders during development and delivery.
1.2 Document Conventions
The keywords “shall”, “must”, and “will” indicate mandatory requirements.
Requirements are uniquely identified using IDs such as REQ-HP-01.
English is used as the primary language of this document.
1.3 Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions
This document is intended for project stakeholders, UI/UX designers, frontend and backend developers, QA engineers, SEO teams, and content managers. Readers should begin with Sections 1 and 2 for context and proceed to Section 4 for detailed functional requirements.
1.4 Product Scope
The PPF website is a responsive landing website aimed at presenting the mission, vision, and impact of the Pak-Palestine Forum. The website shall educate visitors about PPF’s objectives, showcase its geographical presence, encourage engagement through volunteering and donations, support bilingual content in English and Urdu. Phase-1 focuses only on the homepage, with future expansion planned.
1.5 References
The document is based on the IEEE Software Requirements Specification Template, client requirement elicitation notes, and the Global Sumud Flotilla website for FAQ inspiration.
2. Overall Description
2.1 Product Perspective
The PPF website is a new standalone web application accessible through modern browsers. It does not replace any existing system and will serve as the primary digital presence of Pak-Palestine Forum.
2.2 Product Functions
At a high level, the website shall display a multi-section landing page, present organizational vision and impact, provide FAQs, show geographic presence via a static map image, enable volunteering and contacting options, support English and Urdu languages, and highlight call-to-action features.
2.3 User Classes and Characteristics
•	General Visitors shall use the website to seek information about PPF.
•	Volunteers shall use the website to join PPF initiatives. 
•	Media and Organizations shall use the website to verify PPF’s authenticity.
2.4 Operating Environment
The system shall operate on modern web browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. The website shall be accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. The final hosting platform is currently TBD.
REQ-DOM-01: The official domain for the website shall be pakpalforum.com.
REQ-DOM-02: The official contact email for communication shall be pakpalforum@gmail.com.

2.5 Design and Implementation Constraints
The website shall support English and Urdu languages, remain responsive across devices, and align with humanitarian branding standards.
2.7 Assumptions and Dependencies
All required content including text, images, and team details shall be provided by the content manager.
REQ-BRAND-01: The branding colors shall be confirmed by the client before UI implementation.
REQ-BRAND-02: The logo design shall be provided by the client.
REQ-BRAND-03: The font family shall be confirmed by the client.
REQ-BRAND-04: The locations of PPF shall be provided by the client.

3.2 Hardware Interfaces
No hardware interfaces are required.

REQ-COM-01: The system shall use HTTPS for secure communication.

4. System Features
4.1 Homepage & Landing Sections
REQ-HP-01: The system shall display a Hero Section containing images and introductory content about Pak-Palestine Forum.
REQ-HP-02: The system shall include a Vision and Objectives section describing PPF’s mission.
REQ-HP-03: The system shall provide an FAQ section inspired by the Global Sumud Flotilla website addressing common questions.
REQ-HP-04: The system shall display an Our Impact section highlighting PPF’s activities and achievements.
REQ-HP-05: The system shall include a Map section showing PPF’s geographical presence.
REQ-HP-06: The system shall provide a Contact Us section allowing users to volunteer  (join us) or access contact details.
REQ-HP-07: The system shall display team and impactful profiles including names, roles, and images.

4.2 Multilingual Support
REQ-ML-01: The system shall support both English and Urdu languages.
REQ-ML-02: Users shall be able to switch languages at any time.

4.3 Call-to-Action Features
REQ-CTA-01: The system shall visually highlight Join Us, Contact US CTAs throughout the homepage.

5. Other Nonfunctional Requirements
REQ-PERF-01: Pages shall load within 3 seconds on standard internet connections.
REQ-SEC-01: The system shall enforce HTTPS communication.
REQ-SEC-02: The system shall securely handle user data.
REQ-QUAL-01: The system shall provide simple and intuitive navigation.
REQ-QUAL-02: The system shall ensure accessibility through mobile-friendly design.
REQ-QUAL-03: The system shall maintain high availability and modular maintainability.

The system shall follow SEO friendly architecture, and SEO best practices, SEO best practices, including Google Search Essentials, Technical SEO & On-Page SEO Best Practices

5.5 Business Rules
REQ-BR-01: Only developers shall be able to update website content as there is no admin side.

6. Other Requirements
The system shall support future scalability for additional pages and campaigns. The system shall comply with standards.
